Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI)

Teaching Team Contact

The Digital Egypt Pioneers Initiative (DEPI) is a comprehensive program designed to empower Egypt's youth with cutting-edge digital skills and knowledge. Our dedicated teaching team is committed to providing world-class education in data science, machine learning, and artificial intelligence.

Whether you have questions about the program, need technical support, or want to collaborate, we're here to help. Reach out to our instructors using the contact information below.

EYouth Company

I completed the DEPI program through EYouth, and honestly, it was one of the best learning experiences I've ever had. eYouth is a truly professional and highly respectable company. From the very first day, their communication, organization, and level of care were outstanding. The team – from management to instructors and technical support – treats every student with genuine respect and attention.

They respond quickly, follow up on every detail, and make sure you have everything you need to succeed. The whole experience felt smooth, well-structured, and truly supportive. It's rare to find a company in Egypt that combines this level of professionalism, warmth, and commitment to quality.
